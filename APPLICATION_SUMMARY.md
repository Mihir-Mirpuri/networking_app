# Lattice - Application Summary

## What It Does

Lattice is a networking outreach tool designed for students and professionals in finance and consulting. It helps users discover and connect with alumni and professionals at target companies.

## Core Features

**People Discovery**
- Automatically finds professionals who match your search criteria (university, company, role, location)
- Discovers people through public sources like LinkedIn and company websites
- Builds a shared database of discovered professionals that benefits all users

**Email Finding**
- Automatically finds work email addresses for discovered professionals
- Verifies email addresses when possible
- Shows email confidence levels to help you decide who to contact

**Personalized Outreach**
- Generates personalized email drafts using customizable templates
- Supports template tokens like `{first_name}`, `{company}`, `{university}`, `{role}`
- Allows you to review and edit emails before sending
- Option to attach your resume to outreach emails

**Email Sending**
- Send emails directly through your Gmail account
- Schedule emails to be sent at specific times
- Track all sent emails in a history log

**Rate Limits & Safety**
- Daily limit of 30 emails per user to prevent spam
- Batch limit of 10 emails per send operation
- Only verified or manually confirmed emails can be sent
- Full audit trail of all sent emails

## Value Provided

1. **Saves Time**: Automates the tedious process of finding professionals and their contact information
2. **Increases Reach**: Helps you discover more relevant connections than manual searching
3. **Personalization at Scale**: Generates personalized emails quickly while maintaining quality
4. **Collective Knowledge**: Benefits from discoveries made by other users (shared person database)
5. **Professional Outreach**: Ensures you only send to verified email addresses with proper rate limiting
6. **Organization**: Keeps track of all your outreach efforts in one place

## Use Cases

- Students reaching out to alumni for informational interviews
- Job seekers networking with professionals at target companies
- Professionals building relationships in their industry
- Anyone looking to expand their professional network systematically
